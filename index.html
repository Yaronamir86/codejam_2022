ABOUT ME

Every day, we all deal with projects and products of web development, often without even thinking that it's not just a website but a product of long, diligent teamwork.
However, my encounter with web design started not long ago. Having graduated from college as an electrical engineer, I needed to figure out what I want to do next. I am into STEM subjects, and at one point I even wanted to go to graduate school, but later abandoned the idea. During my college days, I used to go to a lot of meetups, and it was at one of these events that I met Elise. d in STEM Well…
Later on over a cup of coffee, it turned out that Elise is a frequent visitor of such events. Moreover, she is an active volunteer of GOG (Google Developer's Group), and it was at a GOG's event that we met. Elise told me about Practicum, a program that she had previously completed herself and then after a while they invited her to work with them as a mentor.
I got so inspired by that program that I couldn't wait to start studying and, more importantly, launching projects that I had been designing for some time already. That's how, seemingly easily, my long road to becoming a web developer began!
